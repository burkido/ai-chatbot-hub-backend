Cookie Policy

How we handle cookies and similar technologies

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies directly in our mobile app, but third-party services like Google AdMob and Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect and analyze data. For more information, please refer to Google's privacy policies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

How Third-Party Services Use Cookies

Our app integrates with the following third-party services that may use cookies or similar technologies:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your interests and to avoid showing you the same ads repeatedly.
  • Google Analytics: Used to understand how users interact with our app. Analytics may use cookies to collect information about your usage patterns.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

While we do not directly control the cookies used by third-party services, you can manage your preferences through your device settings:

  • You can adjust your privacy settings on your mobile device to limit tracking.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's ad settings.
  • For more information about how to manage cookie preferences, please refer to your browser or device documentation.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our business practices. Any changes will be posted on this page, and if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.
