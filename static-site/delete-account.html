Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to permanently delete your Doctor AI account

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your Doctor AI account, please follow these simple steps:

1

Log into your account

Sign in to your Doctor AI account using your credentials

2

Click the profile icon

Look for the profile icon in the top right corner of the page

3

Scroll to the bottom

Navigate to the bottom of your profile settings page

4

Click "Delete Account"

Find and click the "Delete Account" button to permanently remove your account

⚠️ Important Notice

This action cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted:

  • All your personal data will be permanently removed
  • Your medical history and consultations will be deleted
  • You will no longer have access to our services
  • Any active subscriptions will be cancelled
